Welcome!

Hi! I'm Barny, a passionate web developer based in Kent. I specialize in front-end development and creating engaging user experiences.

Discovering My Passion

My journey into the world of web development began unexpectedly. While experimenting with basic HTML and WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) programs, I discovered a fascination with how a few lines of code could create something visually engaging. From that moment on, I was hooked.

Learning the Ropes

Driven by curiosity, I dove deeper into web technologies. I started by learning HTML and CSS, which provided the foundation for building websites. As I created small projects, I realized that each new skill acquired opened doors to new possibilities. The sense of achievement I felt when seeing my projects come to life fueled my determination to learn more.

Embracing Challenges

As I progressed, I encountered challenges that tested my resolve. Debugging code felt overwhelming at times, and I struggled with understanding complex concepts. However, each obstacle taught me resilience. With persistence, I sought help from online communities and resources, turning my challenges into stepping stones for growth.

From Projects to Passion

As time went on, I worked on various projects, from simple personal websites to more complex applications. Each project not only improved my skills but also revealed my love for creating functional, user-friendly designs. Today, I find joy in crafting websites that make a positive impact on users' experiences.

The Future Ahead

As I continue my journey in web development, I remain committed to learning and adapting to the ever-evolving landscape of technology. My goal is to create innovative solutions that empower others and inspire future generations of developers. I'm excited about the path ahead and the opportunities it holds.
